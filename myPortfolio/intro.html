About me

Hello! I'm Yathish Shettigar, from town of Udupi in Karnataka. I hold a degree in B.E. Computer Science and Engineering from East West College of Engineering, Bangalore. My educational journey began at Government Pre University College in Brahmavara, where I pursued my studies in PCMB.

Passionate about the creative realm, I've found my niche in Front-end development and UI/UX Designing. Known technical skills are HTML, CSS, Javascript, bootstrap etc and proficiency in tools like Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Figma, Adobe XD, and Adobe Lightroom, I bring ideas to life through visual storytelling.

I've also gained hands-on experience during a 4-month internship as a Social Media Content Designing and Graphic Designing Intern at Growcify, a Saas platform. This opportunity helped me refine my skills and learn the art of creating impactful visual content.

In addition to my design skills, I'm a multilingual communicator, fluent in Kannada, English, Hindi, and Tulu. I believe in the power of design to connect, inspire, and transform, and I'm excited to continue my journey in the world of creativity and innovation.
